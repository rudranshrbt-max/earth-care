The Purity Process

Bamboo salt is a centuries-old Korean purification technique: raw sea salt is packed into cut bamboo stalks, sealed with mountain clay, and fired repeatedly at extreme heat. Each firing strips impurities and concentrates the trace minerals naturally present in sea salt, leaving behind a coarse, alkaline mineral crystal.

Why Earth&Care

Our salt undergoes nine purification cycles — the traditional benchmark for the highest grade of bamboo salt. It is left unrefined and additive-free, suitable for bathing rituals, body scrubs, and mineral-rich skin contact.
